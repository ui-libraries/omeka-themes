WALTER SERNER

Born Walter Eduard Seligmann.
Born 15 January 1889 in Karlsbad, Austria (now Karlovy Vary, Czech Republic).
Presumed to have died in a German concentration camp (Theresienstadt, Minsk) after 20 Aug. 1942.

Writer associated with the Dada movement in Zurich and Geneva.

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Serner.
