GEORGES RIBEMONT-DESSAIGNES

Born 19 June 1884 in Montpellier, France.
Died 9 July 1974 in Saint-Jeannet, France.

Writer and artist associated with the Dada movement in Paris.

The International Dada Archive has substantial holdings of works by and about Ribemont-Dessaignes.
