HANNAH HÖCH

Born 1 November 1889 in Gotha, Germany.
Died 31 May 1978 in Berlin.

Artist associated with the Dada movement in Berlin.

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Höch. The Archive has microfilmed a large portion of the Hannah-Höch-Nachlass at the Berlinische Galerie.
