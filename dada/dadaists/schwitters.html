KURT SCHWITTERS

Born 20 July 1887 in Hanover, Germany.
Died 8 January 1948 in Ambleside, England.

Artist and writer associated with the Dada movement in Hanover.

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Schwitters. The Archive has microfilmed large portions of the Kurt-Schwitters-Archive at the Stadtbibliothek Hannover.
