CHRISTOF SPENGEMANN

Born 13 April 1877, Hanover.
Died 9 January 1952, Hanover.

Writer and publisher associated with the Dada movement in Hanover.

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Spengemann. The Archive has microfilmed the Spengemann papers at the Stadtbibliothek Hannover.
