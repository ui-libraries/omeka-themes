FRANZ JUNG

Born 26 November 1888 in Neisse, Upper Silesia, Germany (now Nysa, Poland).
Died 21 January 1963 in Stuttgart.

Writer associated with the Dada movement in Berlin.

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Jung.
