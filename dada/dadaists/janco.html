MARCEL JANCO

Born 24 May 1895 in Bucharest.
Died 21 April 1984 near Tel Aviv, Israel.

Artist associated with the Dada movement in Zurich.

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Janco, including a microfilm of the Janco papers in Israel.
