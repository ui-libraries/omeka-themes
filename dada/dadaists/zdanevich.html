ILIAZD ZDANEVICH

Born Ilia Zdanevich.
Born 21 April 1894 in Tbilisi, Russia (now Republic of Georgia).
Died 25 December 1975 in Paris.

Writer and artist associated with the Dada movement in Georgia, Russia, and Paris.

The International Dada Archive has substantial holdings of works by and about Iliazd.
