PHILIPPE SOUPAULT

Born 2 August 1897 in Chaville (Seine-et-Oise), France.
Died 12 March 1990 in Paris.

Writer associated with the Dada movement in Paris.

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Soupault.
