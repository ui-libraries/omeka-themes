VIKING EGGELING

Born 12 October 1880 in Lund, Sweden.
Died 19 May 1925 in Berlin.

Artist and filmaker associated with the Dada movement in Zurich and Berlin.

The International Dada Archive has substantial holdings of works by and about Eggeling.
