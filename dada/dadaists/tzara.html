TRISTAN TZARA

Born Samuel Rosenstock.
Born 16 April 1896 in Moinesti, Romania.
Died 24 December 1963 in Paris.

Writer associated with the Dada movement in Zurich and Paris.

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Tzara.
