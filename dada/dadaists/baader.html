JOHANNES BAADER

Born 22 June 1875 in Stuttgart.
Died 15 January 1955 at Schloss Adelsdorf, Lower Bavaria, Germany.

Writer and artist associated with the Dada movement in Berlin.

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Baader. The Archive has microfilmed the Baader papers in the Jean Brown Collection (now housed at the Getty Foundation).
