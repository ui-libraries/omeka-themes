PAUL ELUARD

Born Eugène Emile Paul Grindel.
Born 14 December 1895 in Saint-Denis, France.
Died 18 November 1952 in Charenton, France.

Writer associated with the Dada movement in Paris.

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Eluard.
