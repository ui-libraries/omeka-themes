JOHANNES BAARGELD

Pseudonym of Alfred Emanuel Ferdinand Gruenwald.

Born 9 October 1892 in Stettin, Germany (now Szczecin, Poland).
Died 18 August 1927 while climbing Aiguille de Bionnassay in the French Alps.

Artist and writer associated with the Dada movement in Cologne.

The International Dada Archive has substantial holdings of works by and about Baargeld.
