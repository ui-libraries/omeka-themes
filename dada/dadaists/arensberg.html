ESLTER CONRAD ARENSBERG

Born 4 April 1878 in Pittsburgh.
Died 29 January 1954 in Los Angeles.

Writer and collector associated with the Dada movement in New York.

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Arensberg. The Archive has microfilmed a large portion of the Arensberg papers housed at the Francis Bacon Foundation in Claremont, California.
