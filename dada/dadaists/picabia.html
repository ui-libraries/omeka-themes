FRANCIS PICABIA

Born Francis-Marie Martinez Picabia.

Born 28 January 1879 in Paris.
Died 30 November 1953 in Paris.

Artist and writer associated with the Dada movement in New York, Zurich, and Paris.

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Picabia.
