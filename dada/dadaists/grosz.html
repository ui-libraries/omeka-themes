GEORGE GROSZ

Born 26 July 1893 in Berlin.
Died 6 July 1959 in Berlin.

Artist associated with the Dada movement in Berlin

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Grosz. The Archive has microfilmed large portions of the Grosz papers at the Akademie der Künste in Berlin and (with permission of the donor) large portions of the Grosz papers donated by Peter Grosz and now housed in the Houghton Library at Harvard University.
