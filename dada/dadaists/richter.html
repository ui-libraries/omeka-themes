HANS RICHTER

Born Johannes Siegfried Richter.
Born 6 April 1888 in Berlin.
Died 1 February 1976 in Minusio/Locarno, Switzerland.

Artist and filmmaker associated with the Dada movement in Zurich.

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Richter. The Archive has microfilmed most of the Hans Richter Archive at the Museum of Modern Art in New York.
