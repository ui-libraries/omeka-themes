JOHN HERZFELD

Born Hellmuth Franz Joseph Herzfeld.
Born 19 June 1891 in Munich.
Died 26 April 1968 in Berlin.

Photomontage artist associated with the Dada movement in Berlin.

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Heartfield.
