CHRISTIAN SCHAD

Born 21 Aug. 1894 in Miesbach (Upper Bavaria), Germany.
Died 25 February 1982 in Stuttgart.

Artist associated with the Dada movement in Zurich.

The International Dada Archive has substantial holdings of works by and about Schad.
