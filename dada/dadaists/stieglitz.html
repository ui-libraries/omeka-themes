ALFRED STIEGLITZ

Born 1 January 1864 in Hoboken, New Jersey.
Died 13 July 1946 in New York.

Photographer, gallery owner, and editor associated with the Dada movement in New York.

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Stieglitz.
