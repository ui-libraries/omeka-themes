WIELAND HERZFELDE

Born Wieland Herzfeld.
Born 11 April 1896 in Weggis, Switzerland.
Died 23 November 1988 in Berlin.

Writer and publisher associated with the Dada movement in Berlin.

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Herzfelde. The Archive has microfilmed portions of the correspondence in the Wieland Herzfelde Malik-Archiv in Berlin.
