EMMY HENNINGS

Born 17 January 1885 in Flensburg, Germany.
Died 10 August 1948 in Lugano-Sorengo, Switzerland.

Writer and performer associated with the Dada movement in Zurich.

The International Dada Archive has substantial holdings of works by and about Hennings. The Archive has microfilmed large portions of the Hugo Ball papers in the possession of Annemarie Schütt-Hennings at Agno, Switzerland, as well as the Hugo-Ball-Sammlung at the Stadtbücherei Pirmasens, Germany. Both of these collections include material relating to Emmy Hennings.
