SOPHIE TAEUBER-ARP

Born 19 January 1889 in Davos, Switzerland.
Died 13 January 1943 in Zurich.

Artist associated with the Dada movement in Zurich.

The International Dada Archive has extensive holdings of works by and about Taeuber-Arp.
